About the App

Photowise: AI Album & Cleanup helps you organize photos into AI albums, clean screenshots and screen recordings in batches, and track cleanup history.

Key Features

  • AI Albums: Group photos by themes such as baby, pets, food, and more.
  • Smart Cleanup: Quickly find screenshots and screen recordings for batch deletion.
  • Cleanup History: Track deleted count and released storage over time.
  • Home Screen Widget: Add a widget to show a selected AI album.
  • Privacy First: Processing is designed to run on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does Photowise do?
A: It helps organize your photos into AI albums and clean screenshots or screen recordings in batches.
Q: Will it delete photos automatically?
A: No. Deletion actions are always user-confirmed. Please review selections before deleting.
Q: Can deleted photos be recovered?
A: Yes. Deleted photos move to Apple's "Recently Deleted" album and can usually be recovered within 30 days.
Q: Does the app require internet?
A: Core features work locally on device. Internet is not required for normal photo processing workflows.
Q: Why is Photo Library access needed?
A: Access is required to read your library for organization/cleanup and to apply user-confirmed write actions.

How to Use

Getting Started

  1. Open Photowise: AI Album & Cleanup.
  2. Grant Photo Library permission when prompted.
  3. Use the Clean tab to review and remove screenshots/screen recordings.
  4. Use the Organize tab to browse AI album categories.
  5. Open History to review cleanup summaries.
