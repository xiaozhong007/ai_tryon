PurePixel Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025
Last Updated: July 1, 2025

Overview

PurePixel is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

PurePixel is designed to operate entirely locally on your device. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your photos or images
  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics
  • Analytics data

Local Processing

All photo analysis and processing occurs locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device or get sent to our servers.

Permission Usage

Photo Library Access Permission

PurePixel requests access to your photo library to:

  • Scan and analyze photo quality
  • Identify duplicate or low-quality images
  • Provide photo organization suggestions

Photo Library Modification Permission

PurePixel requests photo library modification permission to:

  • Delete low-quality photos you choose to remove
  • Move deleted photos to the "Recently Deleted" folder

Important Note: All deletion operations require your explicit confirmation. We never automatically delete any photos.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any personal data, there's no risk of data breaches. All processing occurs securely on your device.

Third-Party Services

PurePixel does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools.

Children's Privacy

PurePixel is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no children's privacy concerns.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, traditional data rights (such as access, correction, deletion) don't apply. You have complete control over the app's access to your photo library and can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you within the app and posted on this page.
